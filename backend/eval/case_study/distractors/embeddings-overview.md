# Embeddings Overview

Everything about embeddings in one overview. How do vector embeddings work? They
map text to dense vectors. What embedding models are popular? OpenAI, Cohere, and
open-source sentence transformers. How do I choose between OpenAI and open-source
embedding models? Trade cost against control. What is Matryoshka representation
learning for embeddings? Nested truncatable dimensions. What is the trade-off when
choosing embedding dimensionality? Accuracy versus storage. How do multilingual
embeddings enable cross-lingual retrieval? A shared vector space. What are the
dimensions of OpenAI embeddings? 1536 for text-embedding-3-small. This overview
summarizes how embeddings work, popular embedding models, dimensionality and
Matryoshka learning, and multilingual cross-lingual retrieval in one embeddings
reference page covering every embedding topic together.
